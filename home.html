kenthack is a hackathon hosted by the University of Kent, run for and by current Kent students.

Interested?

Leave your email address with us and we'll keep you informed of all upcoming kenthack information!

What do you do?

Be creative. Simple as. We get together, and literally spend a day making a project from scratch. Whether it's a one-off joke app for Android, or a full iOS app with a business model and expansion plans, this is the time to get creative!.

You come along, and simply be creative.

All code produced must be fresh during the event, so please don't bring any existing work to kenthack. Existing startups and projects are okay, as long as the "new stuff" are new features / prototypes, so you're not just working on existing stuff.

We may also be having some talks from various individuals on various topics throughout the event - watch this space for more details!

Hackathons are a great place to network and get your name out there. So we'd it if you'd come along!

Why is this happening?

Hackathons are beneficial to students because they encourage students to be creative, and not program just for assessments. A lot of coursework involves programming to solve a problem, or a real-world problem. Which is great. But that restricts a lot of creativity. Some of the best projects, apps, programs, websites, etc. come from people thinking "If only there was an app that did that....

Hackathons are also the perfect time for developers, designers & entrepreneurs to simply be creative. And at the University, we have a wide range of developers in Computer Science courses, a wide range of designers in Graphic Design & Media courses and a wide range of entrepreneurs in Business courses. So it’s about time we get everyone together and come up with the "next big thing"!

Of course, teams may not come up with the next big thing. They could create a little one-off project that gets some laughs and entertains us! And that’s even cooler! :)

Who can come?

For our first year, we're open to any student in the University of Kent Canterbury & Medway campuses. Medway students can get to the Canterbury campus via a shuttle bus that travels between the two campuses.
