Are you A Designer? A Developer? An Entrepreneur? A Startup?

#kenthack is a hackathon based in Canterbury, for students in the Kent area from different backgrounds to collaborate and build something awesome.

And we're back on Friday 7th June.

This is an opportunity to impress people, boost employability & build teams to work on projects over the summer.

You have twelve hours, to either develop new ideas or work on current projects, with your team or with a new team.

We are fortunate enough to be hosting the event at Silicon Aspire, an experimental new working space soon to launch in Canterbury. They are all about turning students into successful professionals.

What's in it for me?

We have some fantastic prizes to give away to the best team, to the most interesting team, and to the funniest team, and so on. We also have individual prizes for individual people within teams too!

If you come first...

We're offering an eight day party package holiday in IBIZA thanks to our sponsor Silicon Aspire, with over 20 other student entrepreneurs from all over the world! This is a unique opportunity to literally come and party with the business icons of tomorrow!

For more details check out this document or see the video below!

If you come second...

You will be offered a place in a team of people working on a new startup (of course with shares!) - more details about this on the day!

And if you come third...

We will be offering a professional-style photo shoot with you and your friends, which would be perfect for your future business cards!

Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be food at the event?
Yup! Our venue is supplying us with some food and goodies for you throughout the event!
Will there be WiFi at the event?
Definitely! Who runs a hackathon on Ethernet?
What do I need to bring?
Your own kit. Your computer, and any devices you wish to develop on (if any!)!
If there are no tickets, can I still come?
Although we have a designated number of tickets, we can let a few more in on the day - just be sure to get there early!
