We came. We saw. We hacked.

#kenthack is a hackathon based in Canterbury, for students in the Kent area from different backgrounds to collaborate and build something awesome.

This is an opportunity to impress people, boost employability & build teams to work on projects over the summer.

You have twelve hours, to either develop new ideas or work on current projects, with your team or with a new team.

We all had an excellent time bouncing ideas around and developing apps at the Canterbury Academy thanks to Silicon Aspire!

It was a great day seeing a mixture of designers, developers & entrepreneurs of all ages come together and work in teams to think up & pitch some apps!

Overall it was a lot of fun and we hope to be doing a similar event in the near future!

We don't know when we'll be back, but be sure to check back here soon :)

Or why not follow us on Facebook and Twitter for the latest updates! :)
